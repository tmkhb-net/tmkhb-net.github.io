TMK Composter supplies a line of commercial composting machines that can compost from 30kg of food waste a day, up to 1350kg of food waste a day. Apart from the line of commercial units, we also offer a large-scale composting machine set up for the Asia market.<\/p>\n

Our machines, when paired with a grinder, and water squeezer, can increase the machine\u2019s daily composting capacity. One such example is a large-scale set in Southeast Asia.
\n\u00a0
\nTMK-5000 composting machine is paired with four accessory pieces:
\n\u00a0
\n1 \u2013 Sorting Platform
\n\u00a0
\n2 \u2013 Oil and Water Separator
\n\u00a0
\n3 \u2013 Shredder
\n\u00a0
\n4 \u2013 Squeezer
\n\u00a0
\nThe TMK-5000 has a daily capacity of 5000kg\/day (5 tonnes). When paired with the above accessories, the machine\u2019s capacity is increased to 8 tonnes a day.
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/a>
\n\t\tSorting Platform<\/strong>
\n\u00a0
\nFood waste is collected in waste hauling trucks and emptied onto the sorting platform. Operators remove contamination like plastics, metals, non-organic materials, and extra-large objects from the food waste. The food waste is then pushed toward the conveyor belt and into the oil and water separator.
\n\u00a0
\nOil and Water Separator<\/strong>
\n\u00a0
\nThe oil and water separator compresses the oil and water from the food waste, to reduce the amount of moisture content within the organic waste. Through a conveyor belt system, the food waste is then moved to the shredder.
\n\u00a0
\nShredder<\/strong>
\n\u00a0
\nThe shredder is used to pulverize food waste to reduce its surface area, thus increasing the machine\u2019s daily composting capacity.
\n\u00a0
\nSqueezer<\/strong>
\n\u00a0
\nApart from small particle size, reducing the water content in the food waste is also crucial to increasing the machine\u2019s composting capacity. The squeezer presses the food waste against a fine mesh screen to remove as much water as possible. The food waste is then transported through a conveyor belt and emptied directly into the composting machine.
\n\u00a0
\nSuch large-scale composting systems are ideal for central food waste collection sites. The food waste is quickly composted in 24 hours, and the reduction rate is 80-90%. The compost can then be screened and used as a soil amendment.
