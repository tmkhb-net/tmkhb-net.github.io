ABOUT TOPMAKE ENVIRONMENT GROUP<\/h3>\n

\t\tTMK<\/strong> aims to transform waste management on a global level, thus contributing to reducing climate change and making the world a better place to live!
\n\u00a0<\/p>\n

The idea of effectively managing waste has been practiced from time immemorial, though, has lost participation from people owing to it being a painstaking process. Your organic house waste shall no longer turn into unhygienic, foul-smelling, hazardous matter polluting the MOTHER EARTH.<\/p>\n

TMK<\/strong> has come up with a unique solution by formulating an organic composting machine that enables users to convert organic waste into extremely effective bio-fertilizers and planting media.\u00a0Built on the principles of continuous active composting, our machines are compact, safe, and easy to handle.<\/p>\n
