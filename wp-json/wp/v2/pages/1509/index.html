TOGETHER, WE CAN<\/h1>\n

#SAYNOTOFOODWASTE\u200b<\/h2>\n

TMK composter is committed to helping the globe achieve zero food waste by providing automatic organic waste composting solutions and systems for all kinds of businesses and homes. Our goal is to help achieve sustainability, allowing the food waste from the farms, restaurants, hotels, food processing plants, etc. to go back to the farms as fertilizer for future food.<\/p>\n

TMK manufactures in China and distributes worldwide a complete line of organic waste compost machines to help solve the problem of food waste disposal. It works with assisted living communities, catering companies, schools, and other public institutions. TMK produces a wide variety of different models of food waste recycling machines, from 2kgs to 300 tons<\/strong>, in order to meet the composting needs of your large or small business.<\/p>\n

Our compost converters also can transfer waste to energy – bio fuel and animal feed.<\/p>\n

Food waste recycling machine increases the speed of the composting process to produce rich, healthy, organic compost in as little as 24 hours. This solution is an alternative to traditional methods of organic waste disposal such as landfill dumping and incineration. Converters reduce environmental pollution and they produce valuable end products in the form of compost. <\/p>\n
